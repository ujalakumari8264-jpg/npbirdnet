## Comprehensive Benefits of Bird Netting

### Category 1: Health and Safety Benefits

#### Disease Prevention

Birds, particularly pigeons, carry over 60 transmissible diseases:

**Major Health Risks:**

- **Histoplasmosis:** Respiratory disease from fungus in droppings
- **Cryptococcosis:** Serious infection affecting lungs and brain
- **Psittacosis:** Bacterial infection causing flu-like symptoms
- **Salmonella:** Food poisoning and gastroenteritis
- **E. coli:** Serious bacterial infections
- **Candidiasis:** Fungal infection

**Parasite Transmission:**

- Bird mites
- Ticks
- Fleas
- Bed bugs (transported by birds)

**Value of Prevention:**

- Medical treatment costs: $500-5,000+ per incident
- Lost work productivity: $200-1,000+ per day
- Long-term health impacts: Priceless
- Peace of mind: Invaluable

**Bird Netting Benefit:** 100% elimination of disease transmission risk from birds on your property

#### Accident Prevention

**Slip and Fall Hazards:**

- Bird droppings create slippery surfaces
- Liability for injuries on your property
- Workers' compensation claims
- Lawsuit potential

**Statistics:**

- Average slip-and-fall settlement: $20,000-50,000
- Legal fees: $10,000-30,000
- Insurance premium increases: 20-40%

**Bird Netting Benefit:** Eliminates droppings, preventing slip-and-fall accidents

#### Respiratory Health

**Air Quality Issues:**

- Dried droppings become airborne particles
- Trigger asthma and allergies
- Respiratory irritation
- Particularly dangerous for children and elderly

**Healthcare Costs:**

- Asthma treatment: $1,000-3,000/year
- Allergy medications: $500-1,500/year
- Emergency room visits: $500-2,000 per visit

**Bird Netting Benefit:** Improves air quality by eliminating bird waste

#### Child and Pet Safety

**Protection for Vulnerable Family Members:**

- Children safe from disease exposure
- Pets protected from parasites
- Balcony safety for toddlers
- Peace of mind for parents

**Value:** Priceless protection for loved ones
